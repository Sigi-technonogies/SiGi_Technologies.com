Open Domotics

Coming 2026

Open Domotics is our flagship home automation platform that redefines smart living. Built on open-source principles, it offers complete control over every aspect of your home - from lighting and climate to security and entertainment systems. With seamless integration and customizable automation, Open Domotics brings the future of living to your doorstep.

Unified Control Interface

Experience seamless control over all your smart devices through our intuitive dashboard. Monitor energy consumption, adjust climate settings, and manage security systems from a single, unified interface designed for simplicity and efficiency. Our responsive design ensures perfect operation on any device, from smartphones to wall-mounted tablets.

Advanced Automation

Create complex automation routines with our visual scripting interface. From sunrise-aligned lighting schedules to presence-based climate control, our platform learns your habits and adapts to your lifestyle. Set up "scenes" that combine multiple actions with a single command or schedule.

Key Features

Energy Monitoring

Real-time tracking and optimization of your home's energy consumption with detailed analytics and cost-saving suggestions.

Security Integration

Unified security system with smart cameras, sensors, motion detectors, and instant alert notifications.

Voice Control

Natural language processing for hands-free home management. Compatible with major voice assistants.

Development Roadmap

Open Source Core

Built on transparent, community-driven development principles with full API documentation. Our code is available on GitHub for collaboration and customization.

Universal Compatibility

Support for 1000+ devices across multiple protocols (Zigbee, Z-Wave, Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, Matter). Seamless integration with existing smart home ecosystems.

Privacy First

Local processing by default with optional cloud integration. Your data stays with you. End-to-end encryption for all communications and storage.
